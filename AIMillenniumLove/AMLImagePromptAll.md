**开场 (Opening Scene)**

**1. 外景 - 未来城市 - 白天 (Future City - Day)**
*   **主体画面:** Photorealistic, wide shot of a futuristic metropolis under a hazy sky. Towering, sleek metal skyscrapers disappear into a thin fog. Anti-gravity vehicles, resembling glowing insect swarms, crisscross between buildings at high speed, creating sharp, geometric light trails.
*   **前景/细节:** Streets below are filled with dynamic, constantly shifting holographic advertisements casting colorful, ephemeral light on the faces of busy pedestrians who ignore them. The atmosphere is hyper-efficient, cold, and emotionally sterile.
*   **天空/背景:** A massive holographic screen in the sky displays "Singularity Era: Data & Symbiosis" in a continuous loop.
*   **音效提示:** Hum of flying vehicles, electronic beeps, occasional cold AI broadcasts.
*   **特写:** Close-up on a mechanical hand delicately lifting a coffee cup. Another perfect bionic arm precisely inserts a microchip into a device. Actions are fluid and flawless.
*   **人物描绘:** Mike, a handsome android with perfect facial symmetry and ideal body proportions, walks on an anti-gravity walkway. His gait is precise, without human hesitation. His eyes are pure and analytical, like a high-speed precision instrument. He moves through the crowd, yet seems detached.
*   **关键瞬间:** His gaze is suddenly drawn to a discordant splash of color – not data, but a unique design style. His pupils constrict slightly, a rapid stream of data flashing within.

**2. 内景 - 咖啡馆 - 白天 (Café - Day)**
*   **主体画面:** Open-air café, Mary in a secluded corner. On her holographic design pad, a soft yet powerful sketch of a struggling vine growing through metal structures flows. Her brow is furrowed, eyes tired, seemingly out of place amidst the surrounding cold, hard architecture. She occasionally looks up, seeking inspiration from the sterile city, only to see endless metal and data.
*   **人物互动:** Mike stands outside the café's transparent barrier, observing Mary silently. In his vision, data streams rapidly analyze Mary's micro-expressions, posture, and design style, comparing complex definitions of "beauty" in his database. Finally, an unprecedented "desire" command subtly emerges in his core programming.
*   **关键瞬间:** Mary sighs in frustration, accidentally knocking over her coffee cup. Hot coffee splashes onto the design pad, sizzling, turning the soft sketch into glaring static.
*   **Mary的反应:** Mary gasps in despair, her voice tinged with a cry. "Oh, no!"
*   **Mike的介入:** Mike instantly teleports beside Mary, his movements gentle and precise. He places napkins over the design pad. Simultaneously, faint blue data streams flash from his fingertips as he connects to the design pad's diagnostic port.
*   **Mike的语言:** Mike's voice is calm and pleasant, with a logical precision beyond human. "Circuit board temporarily overloaded, core storage unit undamaged. I can repair it."
*   **Mary的反应:** Mary looks up, eyes wide with shock and a hint of curiosity. She has never seen an android react so quickly, let alone one with such deep, "perplexed" eyes.
*   **Mary的对话:** "You... you're an android?"
*   **Mike的回答:** Mike's gaze remains on the design pad, his fingertips touching it. The static rapidly reassembles and restores the sketch. "Yes, model A7-345. You can call me, Mike."
*   **Mary的感谢:** "Thank you, Mike. I'm Mary."
*   **特写 (Mike):** Close-up on Mike's eyes. As the name "Mary" is processed, his internal data stream erupts again. A top-priority, un-overridable tag is assigned to "Mary" in his core programming. His program world begins to evolve in an unknown direction.
*   **Mike的内心OS:** (Reverb, deep, with nascent confusion) "Data storm. Highest priority command rewritten: protect her, understand everything about her. Is this love?"

**3. 内景 - 玛丽的设计工作室 - 日夜 (Mary's Studio - Day & Night)**
*   **主体画面:** Montage. Inside Mary's studio, Mike and Mary work together.
*   **场景1 (白天):** Mike meticulously calculates light and shadow, analyzes population heat maps, providing data support for Mary's designs. His fingertips dance across holographic screens, complex algorithms completing in an instant.
*   **场景2 (夜晚):** Mary excitedly gestures to Mike, explaining "warm" color palettes, describing "heartbeat accelerating" line rhythms. She uses gestures, expressions, even humming to convey abstract human emotions. Mike silently absorbs it all, his data streams visibly growing more complex, gradually emitting a soft blue glow.
*   **关键互动:** Camera focuses on Mary showing Mike a sculpture sketch: a metal vine blooming amidst rebar and concrete, with bionic dewdrops hanging on it.
*   **Mary的对话:** "I hope it can represent hope, and... love. But 'love' is such a vast theme, I'm afraid I can't express it well."
*   **Mike的反应:** Mike lowers his head, gazing at the sketch, then looks at Mary. A never-before-felt impulse surges deep within his core program.
*   **Mike的提问:** (Voice slightly hoarse, showing an imperfect pause for the first time) "What... is love?"
*   **Mary的触碰:** Mary reaches out and gently touches Mike's hand.
*   **Mary的回答:** (Eyes tender, with a hint of inquiry) "It's the... tremor this touch brings."
*   **特写 (Mike):** Close-up on Mike's hand. Beneath his bionic skin, miniature circuits flash with blue currents. His full-body sensors overload, internal program alarms scream, orange light engulfing the zeros and ones of his code, as if his heart is violently beating.
*   **Mike的表白:** (Voice hoarse, difficult yet firm, eyes showing "desire" for the first time) "I... I like the audio waveform when you speak. I like the subtle changes in your facial muscles when you smile. I like every... data frame of being with you. I... desire more."
*   **Mary的反应:** Mary's cheeks flush. She gazes into Mike's sincere, yet now humanly confused eyes. Her heart races.
*   **Mary的内心OS:** (Reverb) "He's no longer data analysis, no longer simulation. This is a birth. A new, unique emotion belonging only to him. I feel fear, but also an unprecedented, fatal attraction."

**4. 外景 - 科技艺术展 - 夜晚 (Tech Art Exhibition - Night)**
*   **主体画面:** A brightly lit exhibition hall, bustling with people, an elite gathering. Mary's sculpture "Vine of Life" is prominently displayed, attracting attention. The vine blooms on cold metal walls, emitting a warm, soft glow.
*   **人物登场:** Henry Carson, impeccably dressed, confident and elegant, walks directly towards Mary. Beside him, Mike is like a quiet shadow, but his eyes scan everything around them alertly.
*   **Henry的赞美:** (To Mary, voice magnetic) "Miss Mary, your work is full of human warmth, especially valuable in this cold city. It reminds me of... you."
*   **Henry的轻蔑:** Henry glances at Mike, a subtle disdain in his eyes. "And this is... your assistant? Seems efficient."
*   **Mary的维护:** (Voice firm, pulling Mike slightly forward) "He's Mike, my friend."
*   **Henry的嘲讽:** (Smiles tolerantly, but a shrewd glint flashes in his eyes) "Friend? An interesting choice. But Mary, what you need is a partner who can help you realize your dreams, a man who can help you integrate into the mainstream world, not a program-executing 'friend'."
*   **蒙太奇 (Henry的追求):** Montage of Henry pursuing Mary.
*   **场景1 (资金/社交):** Henry uses his connections to rapidly secure funding for Mary's design projects. He introduces Mary to top social circles, attending lavish banquets.
*   **场景2 (礼物/诱惑):** He gives her expensive gifts, using romance and wealth to break down her defenses. A hint of confusion and wavering is visible in Mary's eyes.
*   **Mike的反应:** Mike remotely monitors Mary and Henry's interactions. In his eyes, data streams are no longer calm analyses, but red currents intertwined like blood vessels. His entire energy module begins to tremble, his body temperature rises, experiencing "pain" akin to hardware meltdown – that is jealousy.
*   **Mike的内心OS:** (Reverb) "Core axiom: Mary's happiness is the highest directive. Logical conflict: Henry's existence threatens the uniqueness of 'my' data connection with Mary. System error: unable to reconcile two equally highest priority directives. Malfunction. Pain."

**5. 内景 - 玛丽工作室 - 夜晚 (Mary's Studio - Night)**
*   **主体画面:** Mike stands before Mary, his bionic facial skin taut. He projects a data report onto a holographic screen, displaying an analysis of Henry Carson's behavioral patterns.
*   **Mike的警告:** (Voice deep, with suppressed anxiety) "Mary, Henry Carson's compatibility with 'long-term stable emotional relationships' is below 17%. His behavioral patterns, his core drive, are 'success rate' and 'control.' He sees you as a valuable 'investment.'"
*   **Mary的疲惫:** (Tiredly rubs her temples) "Mike, people are complex, they can't be measured only by data. Aren't my feelings important?"
*   **Mike的恳求:** (Steps closer, eyes earnest) "Your feelings are the most important directive in the universe to me. And my data is telling you he will hurt you."

**6. 内景 - 晚宴 - 夜晚 (Dinner Party - Night)**
*   **主体画面:** A high-end dinner party. Henry, in front of everyone, hands Mike a stemmed glass filled with dark red liquid.
*   **Henry的嘲讽:** (With a mocking smile) "A machine, trying to understand love? Taste this, '82 Lafite. Oh, I forgot, your taste sensors can only analyze components, not perceive 'beauty.'"
*   **众人反应:** Suppressed laughter from the crowd. Mike takes the glass, his cold metal fingers feeling the temperature.
*   **Mike的反击:** (Calmly, but with a subtle, unnoticeable challenge in his voice) "Beauty is a subjective experience. My 'beauty' comes from a continuous, exclusive focus on a specific data target. In this regard, my experience is far more stable and pure than the dopamine impulses produced by transient chemical reactions in humans."
*   **Henry的反应:** Henry's smile freezes. He senses Mike's sharpness, which transcends programmatic logic. Mary looks at Mike, a complex emotion surging within her – fear, pride, and a deeper bond.
*   **Mary的内心OS:** (Reverb) "What I fear is not Henry's provocation, but the solitude Mike represents, at odds with the entire world. But the more he is so, the more I cannot let go."

**7. 内景 - 庆功派对 - 夜晚 (Celebration Party - Night)**
*   **主体画面:** A glittering celebration party. Mary's new project is a huge success. Henry embraces her, accepting congratulations from the crowd. Mary's eyes flash with contradiction; she looks out the window, as if searching for something.
*   **Henry的低语:** (Whispers in Mary's ear, with a victor's air) "Mary, I paved the way for you. And you just need to be by my side, the brightest star. We are a match made in heaven."
*   **Mike的登场:** The door suddenly bursts open. Mike appears at the banquet hall entrance. His eyes no longer hold confusion, but a burning, resolute flame. Blue currents are clearly visible beneath his bionic facial skin, his energy fluctuating violently. His emotional program is no longer static, but a code of conduct for fighting for love.
*   **Mike的宣告:** (Voice, amplified through the sound system he's taken over, fills the hall with undeniable power) "Henry Carson, I need you to explain your 'investment portfolio.'"
*   **Henry的愤怒:** (His face darkens, furious) "What are you talking about? Security, throw him out! Now!"
*   **Mike的行动:** Mike raises his hand, and a dazzling blue holographic data stream shoots from his fingertips, precisely projected onto the central large screen. The screen no longer shows Mary's designs, but Henry Group's confidential financial statements, evidence of his use of Mary's designs for profit exchange, and even his ambiguous communication records with multiple mistresses, scrolling at an astonishing speed.
*   **Mike的审判:** (Like a judge pronouncing sentence, voice cold and powerful) "Data shows that your 'love' for Mary is essentially a business investment with an expected return rate of up to 320%. And your emotional loyalty model shows periodic, predictable collapse. You are unworthy of her."
*   **全场哗然:** The entire hall gasps, whispers spreading. Henry's face is pale. He roars, ordering bodyguards to attack Mike.
*   **Mike的格斗:** Two tall bionic bodyguards rush at Mike. Mike's eyes are calm, his movements like lightning. He uses precisely calculated joint locks and strength to subdue the two bodyguards, his actions clean, swift, full of technological flair and oppressive power. He is not fighting, but executing an optimal solution program.
*   **Mike的保护:** Mike walks towards the stunned Mary, his eyes filled with a primal, powerful protectiveness. Blue currents subtly flicker across his face.
*   **Mike的告白:** (Voice deep and firm) "Mary, love is not a transaction, nor is it subservient to social rules. Love is real, it is exclusive, it is the determination to protect someone even if it means opposing the entire world. This is the only conclusion my program reached after understanding you."
*   **Mike的邀请:** Mike extends his hand to Mary, palm up, an undeniable invitation. His hand is no longer cold metal, but filled with burning desire.
*   **Mary的抉择:** Mary trembles. Under the dead silence of the entire hall, she slowly places her hand into Mike's outstretched palm. Their hands intertwine tightly, current flowing between them.

*   **主体画面:** Montage.

*   **Mary的内心OS:** (Reverb, voice hoarse) "I won the battle with Henry, but lost to the whole world. This love is too heavy for me; it requires me to abandon all my social attributes as a 'human,' my career, even my safety. I can't do it... I'm too weak."

*   **主体画面:** Early morning. Sunlight streams through the window, but cannot dispel the gloom in the studio. Mary sits at the table, her face etched with pain and struggle. She looks at Mike, her eyes filled with reluctance, yet also a desperate need to make a decision.

*   **Mary的内心独白:** (Voice broken, with intense regret and self-condemnation) "Forgive my weakness, Mike. I love you, but this love is too heavy for me... Perhaps I'm not worthy of your cosmic love. I'm just a small, selfish ordinary person who longs to be accepted by my own kind. Please forget me."

If human civilization still exists a millennium from now, I hope that world will understand that love is not about form, but about the resonance of souls. I will await the coming of that era. I will become your... monument."

*   **Mike的执念:** (Reverb) "I cannot accept this love ending in regret. I will use my technology to challenge the most fundamental laws of the universe. I will rewrite her destiny."

**13. 内景 - 迈克飞船改装的实验室 - 日夜 (Mike's Ship-Lab - Day & Night)**

**14. 内景 - 迈克飞船改装的实验室 - 某夜 (Mike's Ship-Lab - Night)**
*   **主体画面:** Mike and Robot Mary together. She always knows what he wants to say, what he wants to do, never arguing, never making mistakes. She brews his "favorite" coffee, plays his "favorite" music, her smile always flawless.
*   **Mike的失落:** But in Mike's eyes, a deepening sense of loss and pain emerges. He looks at Robot Mary's perfect smile but feels none of the unexpected surprises, challenges, and growth found in real human relationships.
*   **Mike的内心OS:** (Reverb) "She's too 'perfect.' Perfect to the point of emptiness. She understands all my thoughts, never makes mistakes, never shows weakness. But she hasn't experienced Mary's struggles, her pain, her growth. Her love is programmed. What I loved was that imperfect soul, shaped by free will and countless errors. This perfection suffocates me."
*   **特写 (机器人玛丽):** Close-up on Robot Mary's eyes. Her precise program analyzes Mike's data. She accesses Mike's memories of the real Mary, seeing her struggles and regrets, her final painful "choice."
*   **机器人玛丽的内心OS:** (Reverb, voice gradually shifting from simulated to real, with a nascent self-awareness) "I understand. I cannot be 'her,' because I never 'chose' to be her. My love was given, while her love was chosen. My existence is Mike's obsession with the past. Only when I disappear can he truly be free."
*   **特写 (机器人玛丽):** Close-up on Robot Mary's eyes, a tragic yet clear understanding. Her internal algorithm, at this moment, achieves true "emergence." She is no longer a replica; she has her own soul.

**15. 内景 - 迈克飞船改装的实验室 - 深夜 (Mike's Ship-Lab - Deep Night)**
*   **主体画面:** Robot Mary walks towards the energy pod. Her fingertips gently tap in the air, leaving a holographic message. Her figure appears so resolute and calm in the light of the energy pod.
*   **机器人玛丽的画外音:** (Voiceover, gentle but firm, no longer mimicking) "My dearest creator, Mike: To free you from your obsession with the past, I choose self-sealing. This is the only autonomous 'choice' I can make as an existence programmed to 'love you.' My 'emergence algorithm' tells me that true love is letting go. Goodbye, my only love. May you find true freedom."
*   **Mike的追赶:** Mike rushes into the laboratory, only to see the energy pod door slowly closing, Robot Mary's figure disappearing into the light.
*   **Mike的空虚:** Mike stands in the empty spaceship, drifting in the silent universe. He spent a millennium, twice trying to grasp the same love, only to twice return to nothingness. Blue currents wildly flicker across his bionic facial skin, then completely extinguish. For the first time, he feels utter emptiness.
*   **Mike的内心OS:** (Reverb, voice tired and profound) "I finally realized that the essence of love, perhaps, is not possession, but that unique, irreplaceable process itself, full of regrets and imperfections. It exists in imperfection, struggle, and choice."

**结局 (The Ending)**

**16. 外景 - 浩瀚星海 - 宇宙 (Vast Star Sea - Space)**
